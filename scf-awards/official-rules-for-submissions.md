# Official Rules for Submissions

Please note that each program has its own specific rules, which govern participation in that program, while the following rules apply to all SCF programs and initiatives, including SCF supporting programs (collectively, the “Official Rules”) All of these rules help define the relationship between you and Stellar Development Foundation (“SDF”, “we”, or “us”). By submitting or resubmitting any interest forms, project proposals, or applications, or otherwise participating in any SCF programs or initiatives, you (the “Participant(s)”, “you”, or “yours”) represent and warrant that you have read and understood these rules, have consulted any necessary independent advisors, and agree to comply with all applicable terms and requirements, including the Official Rules.

### 1. Participant Eligibility

**1.1 Each SCF Award Program or Initiative is open to:**

* Teams of Eligible Individuals, as defined below in Section 1.3 (“Teams”); and
* Organizations (including corporations, not-for-profit corporations, and other nonprofit organizations, limited liability companies, partnerships, and other legal entities) that consist of Eligible Individuals and that are duly organized or incorporated and in good standing at the time of submission (“Organizations”).

In any given SCF Award Program or Initiative, an Eligible Individual may not join more than one Team or Organization, and an Eligible Individual who is part of a Team or Organization may not also enter submission for other SCF Awards on an individual basis.

**1.2 If a Team or Organization is entering an SCF Award Program or Initiative:**

The Team or Organization must appoint and authorize one Eligible Individual (the “Representative”) to represent, act, and enter a submission on their behalf. By entering a submission to the SCF Award Program or Initiative on behalf of a Team or Organization, you are representing and warranting that you are the Representative authorized to act on behalf of your Team or Organization.

Your Team or Organization must appoint and authorize at least 2 Eligible Individuals to be available during the preparation webinars, actively participate in bootcamp activities, and should you be interested and get selected as one of the finalists, present during the investor demo day.

**1.3 An “Eligible Individual” is a natural person who:**

* Is 18 years of age or older;
* Is not a citizen of, located in, or otherwise normally residing in Cuba, Iran, North Korea,any Russian-controlled region of Ukraine, or any other country or region subject to sanctions by the US Department of Treasury Office of Foreign Asset Control (“OFAC”), as may be updated from time to time;
* is not identified on the Specially Designated Nationals or Blocked Persons list provided by OFAC; and
* Does not reside in a jurisdiction where the transfer and holding of cryptocurrency is illegal or would require a special license or authorization that the such person does not possess.

### 2. Submission Eligibility&#x20;

Applicable law and our internal policies restricts SDF from providing financial or promotional support to  certain categories of teams and companies. A team or company will be ineligible to participate in SCF or receive an SCF Award if they are or offer products/services that:

* violate any applicable laws, regulations, orders, judgements or rules; infringe or violate any proprietary right of any party, including patent, trademark, trade secret, copyright, right of privacy, right of publicity, or other rights;
* involve, facilitate, support, or promote unlawful activities, including, but not limited to: fraud, drug trafficking, purchases on dark web markets, theft, illegal arms trafficking, human trafficking, abduction, extortion, embezzlement, corruption of public officials, acts of terrorism or terrorist financing, and intellectual property violations;
* involve, facilitate, or promote gambling;
* are offensive, deceptive, harmful, or libelous;
* are/ or contain sexually obscene content;
* are discriminatory or abusive toward any individual or group;
* contain, transmit, or instill malware or phishing pages or devices;
* are primarily focused on promoting a specific token or a specific NFT or NFT collection with no real-world use case;
* are primarily focused on use cases that do not contribute to the growth and development of Soroban, the Stellar network, the Stellar ecosystem, and protocol (e.g., relying on Stellar for arbitrary data storage).
* fail to meet any additional eligibility requirements, milestones, or program-specific rules established for a particular award program, or materially deviate from such requirements.

If you are unsure whether your company or team falls into one or more of these categories, please contact us at communityfund@stellar.org. SDF reserves the right, in its absolute discretion, to disqualify any Participant immediately, without warning or any prior notice, from any current or future SDF programs, and to ban them from future SDF funding programs, if they breach the Official Rules or any applicable law, or act in a way that is inappropriate, not in the best interests or spirit of the SDF programs or otherwise in bad faith, including, without limitation, any direct or indirect tampering with or manipulation of the entry, or abusive behavior towards mentors and other participants.

### 3. Restrictions on Use of Funds

You agree that SDF shall not be obligated to make or deliver any Awards to you if you (i) are reasonably suspected by SDF to have performed or engaged in unlawful activity, such as fraud, wash trading, or market manipulation; or (ii) use any part of any Award, directly or indirectly, to engage in: (a) speculation, wash trading, short selling, insider trading, spoofing, price fixing, market manipulation or other unlawful, illegal, fraudulent, deceptive or manipulative activity related to or involving Stellar lumens (“XLM”) or any other Stellar-based digital asset; (b) providing investment advice, including without limitation, statements on potential profitability or value appreciation of XLM or any other Stellar-based digital asset; (c) marketing or promoting any product or feature that promises interest, a dividend, or other appreciation, yield, or return on XLM; (d) short selling XLM or manipulating the price or volume of XLM, or causing, influencing, or incentivizing the short selling of XLM or manipulating the price or volume of XLM; (e) using XLM for any public marketing activities without the prior consent of SDF in each instance; (f) facilitating or furthering any transactions or engagement with Sanctioned Jurisdictions or Sanctioned Persons (each as defined below); or (g) engaging, directly or indirectly, in any activities in connection with anti-money laundering and/or terrorism financing, or any other acts in breach or contravention of any applicable laws, including Sanctions laws as defined below (the activities described in clauses (i) and (ii) collectively, the “Restricted Uses”).&#x20;

### 4. Sanctions Compliance

For purposes of these Legal Acknowledgements, “Sanctions” mean all economic or financial sanctions or trade embargoes imposed, administered, or enforced by the United States (including U.S. Treasury Department’s Office of Foreign Assets Control (“OFAC”) and the U.S. Department of State), European Union, United Kingdom, United Nations, or other relevant sanctions authority. “Sanctioned Jurisdiction” means any jurisdiction that is the target of comprehensive Sanctions, including as of the date hereof, Cuba, Iran, North Korea, and Russian-controlled areas of Ukraine (currently, Crimea, so-called Donetsk People’s Republic, and so-called Luhansk People’s Republic). “Sanctioned Person” means any individual, entity, organization, or government: (i) designated on a Sanctions-related list maintained by the United States (including OFAC’s List of Specially Designated Persons and Blocked Persons), European Union (including the Consolidated list of persons, groups and entities subject to EU financial sanctions), United Kingdom (including the UK Sanctions List), United Nations, and any other relevant Sanctions authority; (ii) subject to a blocking order by the United States or a relevant Sanctions authority; (iii) located, organized, or ordinarily resident in a Sanctioned Jurisdiction; or (iv) owned or controlled (directly or indirectly) by any individual, entity, organization, or government described in clauses (i), (ii), and (iii).&#x20;

You represent and warrant to SDF that neither you nor any of your directors, officers, shareholders, or agents are Sanctioned Persons.&#x20;

**5.6 Valuation of XLM**

To determine the number of XLM equal to the USD value of any Award, the USD valuation of XLM shall be calculated using the CF Stellar Lumens-Dollar Settlement Price as administered, maintained, and reported by the cryptocurrency index provider CF Benchmarks Ltd. (using the ticker “XLMUSD\_RR”) (available at https://www.cfbenchmarks.com/indices/XLMUSD\_RR), or, if such settlement price is unavailable or reasonably suspected by SDF to be unreliable, the settlement price as reported on a substantially similar and equally reputable cryptocurrency index provider as determined by the SDF in its discretion. The USD valuation of XLM for any particular Award shall be calculated using the CF Stellar Lumens-Dollar Settlement Price (or other agreed upon settlement price) reported on the day such Award is paid. The Participants acknowledge and understand that XLM is a highly risky and volatile asset, and that SDF does not provide any representations, warranties, or guarantees of its value.
